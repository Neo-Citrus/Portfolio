Hi! My name's <b>Eddie</b> and I'm a Spanish illustrator located in Sweden.

I'm originally from the <b>Canary Islands</b>, but ended up moving to Sweden because of a man 🙄 (I'm kidding I love him dearly 🤭). I really enjoy it here! I prefer the cold over the Canarian heat.

Since I was a toddler I've always enjoyed videogames just like my mother did. I grew up playing <i>Spyro</i> on the PS1 and <i>Animal Crossing</i> on the DS Lite. I remember vividly booting up the DS and spending hours running around and talking to my villagers, and trying to complete the Museum.

I think my view on videogames changed when I played <i>Super Mario Galaxy</i> for the very first time. Something about the soundtrack and the environtment had me so in awe. I would just go to Rosalina's observatory and sit and listen to the music while exploring the place. It was a calming presence in my not so calm life.

Another gamed that changed me was <i>Shadow of the Colossus</i>. This is one I actually played recently since I didn't have the game for PS2. And boy was I missing out! The game mechanics were so ahead of its time and even now there's not really anything else like it. And again, the environtment, but especially the Colossi designs took my breath away.

And that's exactly what I want to achieve making games. I want to make people <b>immersed</b> in my games and have fun playing them. And most important for me is for them to <b>feel</b> something when they play my games. To be comforted, especially in these weird and confusing times we're currently living in.
